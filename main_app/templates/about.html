About Lexicon

Lexicon is not your regular flashcards app.

Have you ever heard someone say a word you didn't know the meaning of, and to avoid this moment of embarrassment, you nodded your head as if you understood?

Our mission is to expand your lexicon, so you never have to face this feeling again. We believe you can become your most articulate self with just a few minutes per day.

This open-source app allows users to post their favorite unique and unusual vocabulary words. Feel free to browse the cards and learn them as you go. Try your best to incorporate a few of them in your language each day, and we're confident you'll notice an improvement in your overall lexicon.

We strongly encourage you to contribute to this project as it is fundamental to your learning.
